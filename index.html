What is Crab Island?

Crab Island is a sandbar located just South of the Marler Bridge in Destin, FL. You may have seen it while crossing into Destin, or from 500ft up on a Destin Parasail tour. Crab Island started to gain in popularity as boaters used the area to moor their boats to catch rays and wade in the sand bar. Now Crab Island in Destin, FL, has erupted into a popular social scene where locals and tourists congregate!
