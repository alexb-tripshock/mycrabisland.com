Things to Do Near Crab Island in Destin, FL

From HarborWalk Village to parasailing, here’s how to round out your trip with the top tours, attractions, and family-friendly fun in Destin.

Crab Island Activities in Destin, FL

Crab Island is more than just a floating party—it’s the heart of Destin’s adventure scene. From parasailing high above the harbor to spotting dolphins on a sunset cruise, there are tons of ways to make the most of your day before or after visiting the sandbar. This guide highlights the top things to do near Crab Island, including popular tours, family-friendly attractions, and outdoor adventures that showcase the best of the Emerald Coast. Whether you’re planning a chill float day or packing your trip with activities, we’ve got you covered. Looking for boat rentals or Crab Island tours? Click here to see the top options.

HarborWalk Village

If you're near the harbor, you're near the action. HarborWalk Village is packed with waterfront dining, boutique shopping, live music, ziplining, and entertainment for all ages. It's also the launch point for most Crab Island tours, so you can grab a bite, catch a sunset, or explore the boardwalk before or after your day on the water.

Explore HarborWalk Village

Parasailing Tours

Ready to see Crab Island from above? Parasailing takes you 400 feet up for a breezy, peaceful ride with jaw-dropping views of Destin, the Gulf, and the sandbar below. Take off from the harbor, glide over the water, and check “epic aerial view” off your vacation bucket list.

Book a Parasailing Tour

Dolphin Cruises

Slow things down with a relaxing cruise that gets you up close to Destin’s favorite locals—bottlenose dolphins. These tours are calm, scenic, and perfect for all ages. Most routes pass by Crab Island before heading into the Gulf, and many include narration, snacks, and shaded seating.

Book a Dolphin Cruise

Sunset Cruises

End your day in the best way possible: on the water, golden hour glowing, dolphins playing, music drifting on the breeze. Sunset cruises offer a calm and scenic vibe with bonus photo ops and serious "wish-you-were-here" energy. A favorite for couples and families alike.

Book a Sunset Cruise

Gulfarium Marine Adventure Park

This beloved spot on Okaloosa Island combines education and excitement with dolphin shows, animal encounters, and marine exhibits. Perfect for families or anyone who wants a low-key, high-fun outing close to Destin. Bonus: it’s indoors and shaded when you need a sun break.

Get Gulfarium Tickets

Destin Fishing Charters

Whether you're chasing trophy fish or just want to try something new, Destin delivers. Inshore and deep-sea charters offer the chance to reel in red snapper, grouper, and more. All gear is included, and most trips leave from right near the harbor. It's a must for adventure-seekers.

Book a Fishing Charter
